# Privacy Policy — Yes and No Autism Talk

**Effective date:** 13 May 2026
**Last updated:** 13 May 2026

## Summary

Yes and No Autism Talk is an Augmentative and Alternative Communication (AAC) tool. It collects no personal data, contains no analytics, displays no advertising, and operates entirely on your device.

## Data collection

This app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data of any kind. Specifically:

- No accounts or sign-up
- No analytics (Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, or otherwise)
- No advertising identifiers
- No location data
- No microphone or camera access
- No contacts or other on-device data
- No usage telemetry

## On-device storage

The app stores user preferences (selected theme, voice rate, layout choice, etc.) locally on the device using Android's SharedPreferences. This data never leaves the device and is removed when the app is uninstalled.

## Permissions

The app requests one permission:

- **VIBRATE** — used to give a brief haptic confirmation when a button is tapped. This permission does not enable any data collection.

## Third-party services

The app uses the Android operating system's built-in text-to-speech engine to read "Yes" or "No" aloud. This processing happens on the device. The app makes no network requests during normal use.

The font used in the app is loaded from Google Fonts on first launch. After that initial load, Google Fonts is cached by Android and no further network requests are made.

## Children

The app may be used by children. Because it collects no data of any kind, it complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations by design.
